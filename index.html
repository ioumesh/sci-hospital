SCI Spine Treatment

Selective Nerve Root Desensitization

Selective Nerve Root Desensitization is a specialized procedure that targets specific nerve roots to alleviate chronic inflammation or irritation using advanced radio frequency technology.

Stitchless Endoscopic Spine Procedures

Experience immediate relief from slipped discs, sciatica, and back pain with our stitchless endoscopic spine procedures performed under local anesthesia for optimal results.

Percutaneous Stenoscopic Lumbar Decompression

We offer Percutaneous Stenoscopic Lumbar Decompression, a minimally invasive endoscopic surgery for spinal canal stenosis. This procedure relieves the pressure on spinal nerves by eliminating the compressions in the spinal canal.

Epidural Adhesiolysis

Epidural Adhesiolysis is a highly specialized intervention for patients with nerve entrapment in scar tissue after spine surgery. Our experts navigate the spine using advanced imaging technology to provide precise treatment.
